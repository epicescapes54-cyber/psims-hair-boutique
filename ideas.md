<idea>
**Design Movement:** Art Deco Noir Luxe

**Core Principles:**
1. Deep obsidian black as the dominant canvas, with rose gold as the accent metal
2. Geometric ornamentation — thin line borders, chevron dividers, diamond motifs
3. Asymmetric editorial layout with dramatic typographic scale contrast
4. Every surface has texture: subtle grain, metallic shimmer on rose gold elements

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: Near-black (#0A0A0A, #111111)
- Rose Gold: #B76E79 → #E8A598 gradient spectrum
- Champagne White: #F5EFE6 for body text
- Deep Burgundy: #4A1020 as a secondary accent
- Emotional intent: opulence, mystery, desire — the feeling of entering an exclusive salon

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Full-bleed hero with diagonal split: left half dark, right half a large editorial hair photo
- Staggered product grid with alternating image/text panels
- Sticky side navigation on desktop, drawer on mobile
- Horizontal scrolling "featured collections" strip

**Signature Elements:**
1. Thin rose gold rule lines (1px) used as section dividers and card borders
2. Monogram "PSB" mark rendered in Art Deco typeface as a watermark/logo badge
3. Subtle gold particle animation in the hero background

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Hover reveals: product cards lift with a rose gold border glow
- Smooth parallax scrolling on hero images
- Cursor changes to a custom rose gold dot on interactive elements

**Animation:**
- Hero text: staggered letter-by-letter fade-up on load
- Section entrances: slide-in from left/right alternating
- Product cards: scale 1.02 + shadow bloom on hover
- Rose gold shimmer sweep on CTA buttons

**Typography System:**
- Display: "Playfair Display" — serif, high contrast, editorial authority
- Body: "Cormorant Garamond" — elegant, refined, readable
- Accent/Labels: "Josefin Sans" — geometric, all-caps, Art Deco feel
- Hierarchy: 72px display → 36px section heads → 18px body → 11px labels
</idea>
